# Sorry, I Can’t Hear You – I’m Biased: Demographic Distortions in Text-to-Speech Systems
Dataset and supplementary materials from study about bias in TTS generators.



## Dataset details
Dataset in the study consists of the real and fake speech of celebrities in different languages, with respect to their age and sex. Due to the GDPR and licence agreements it is unethical to publish synthetically generated speech of these people. 

We publish a metadata.csv file with links leading to the videoclips used by us in the research. Each person has 5 associated various materials.


## Social study 
In the first release (v1.0) of this study we tested Polish and English speakers. These studies where disjoint and details of them are presented separately. The recruitment process took place on-line as the study was an on-line form. The study was anonymous and volounteers were not paid.
